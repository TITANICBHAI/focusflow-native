Data Subject Rights

数据主体权利

Your data rights, clearly explained.

您的数据权利，清晰说明。

FocusFlow stores all your data locally on your device — nothing is ever sent to our servers. This page explains your rights and how to exercise them.

FocusFlow 将您的所有数据本地存储在您的设备上——任何内容都不会发送到我们的服务器。本页说明您的权利及如何行使这些权利。

Last updated: May 2026

最后更新：2026年5月

Your rights at a glance

您的权利一览

🔍

Right to Access

访问权

You can view all data FocusFlow stores at any time directly inside the app — tasks, block lists, stats, and settings.

您可以随时直接在应用内查看 FocusFlow 存储的所有数据——任务、屏蔽列表、统计数据和设置。

✏️

Right to Correction

更正权

Edit or update any information (tasks, rules, preferences) at any time from within the app.

随时在应用内编辑或更新任何信息（任务、规则、偏好设置）。

🗑️

Right to Deletion

删除权

Delete individual records in the app, or wipe all data instantly via Android Settings → Apps → FocusFlow → Clear Data, or by uninstalling the app.

在应用内删除单个记录，或通过 Android 设置 → 应用 → FocusFlow → 清除数据立即清除所有数据，或通过卸载应用来删除。

📦

Right to Portability

可携带权

Export a full backup of your data at any time using the Backup & Restore feature inside FocusFlow Settings.

随时使用 FocusFlow 设置中的备份与恢复功能导出您数据的完整备份。

🚫

Right to Object

反对权

Revoke any Android permission (Accessibility, Usage Stats, Draw over Other Apps) at any time in Android Settings. The affected features will stop collecting that data.

随时在 Android 设置中撤销任何 Android 权限（无障碍功能、使用情况统计、显示在其他应用上层）。受影响的功能将停止收集该数据。

🔒

No Server Data

无服务器数据

FocusFlow has no backend database. There is no account, no cloud sync, and no personal data stored on TBTechs servers — ever.

FocusFlow 没有后端数据库。没有账户，没有云同步，TBTechs 服务器上永远不会存储任何个人数据。

What data FocusFlow stores

FocusFlow 存储的数据

  • Tasks & schedules — stored on-device in SQLite.
  • App block lists & allowlists — stored on-device in SQLite and SharedPreferences.
  • Focus session history & statistics — stored locally; never transmitted.
  • App settings & preferences — stored in Android SharedPreferences.
  • Blocked-app intercept log — stored locally; viewable and clearable inside the app.
  • Custom overlay wallpaper (if set) — stored in app-private storage on your device.
  • 任务和日程——存储在设备上的 SQLite 中。
  • 应用屏蔽列表和允许列表——存储在设备上的 SQLite 和 SharedPreferences 中。
  • 专注会话历史和统计数据——本地存储；从不传输。
  • 应用设置和偏好——存储在 Android SharedPreferences 中。
  • 被屏蔽应用拦截日志——本地存储；可在应用内查看和清除。
  • 自定义覆盖层壁纸（如已设置）——存储在设备上的应用私有存储中。

How to delete your data right now

如何立即删除您的数据

  • Individual records: Delete tasks, block rules, or logs directly inside the FocusFlow app.
  • All app data: Android Settings → Apps → FocusFlow → Storage → Clear Data. This is instant and permanent.
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling FocusFlow removes all associated data from your device.
  • 单个记录：直接在 FocusFlow 应用内删除任务、屏蔽规则或日志。
  • 所有应用数据：Android 设置 → 应用 → FocusFlow → 存储 → 清除数据。此操作即时且永久。
  • 卸载：卸载 FocusFlow 会从您的设备中删除所有相关数据。

Third-party data sharing

第三方数据共享

FocusFlow does not share any data with third parties. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising networks, crash-reporting services, or cloud APIs embedded in the app.

FocusFlow 不与第三方共享任何数据。应用中没有嵌入分析 SDK、广告网络、崩溃报告服务或云 API。
