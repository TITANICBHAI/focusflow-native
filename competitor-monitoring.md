## 30-Minute Monthly Ritual

Run this once per month. Paste **"Run my monthly competitive check for [competitor name]"** to trigger an on-demand update.

| Time | Task | What to look for |
|---|---|---|
| 0–5 min | Check Google Alert digest | New press, blog posts, funding announcements since last month |
| 5–10 min | Open Play Store listing | Version updates? Read 3 most recent 1-2 star reviews for new complaint patterns |
| 10–15 min | Open their changelog or blog | Feature launches — note category: pricing, UX, enforcement, AI |
| 15–20 min | Scan LinkedIn jobs | 3+ ML/AI roles = AI feature coming. Sales hire = moving upmarket |
| 20–25 min | Check pricing page | Tier changes, price increases, new plan names |
| 25–30 min | Update change log below | Log date + what changed. Flag anything needing a FocusFlow response |
